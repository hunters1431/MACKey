Privacy Policy

MACKey collects nothing. It all stays on your Mac.

Last updated: June 2026

In one line: MACKey runs entirely on your Mac, makes no network connections, and no data ever leaves your device.
  • 简体中文：MACKey 不收集任何数据，纯本地运行，不联网、不上传，所有数据只留在你的 Mac。
  • 繁體中文：MACKey 不收集任何資料，純本地運行，不聯網、不上傳，所有資料只留在你的 Mac。
  • 日本語：MACKey はいかなるデータも収集しません。すべてローカルで動作し、通信を行いません。
  • Deutsch: MACKey sammelt keinerlei Daten. Es läuft vollständig lokal; keine Daten verlassen deinen Mac.

What we collect

Nothing. MACKey has no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no accounts, and no advertising. We do not see, transmit, or store any information about you or your usage.

Data stored on your device

Your shortcut bindings and theme choice are saved locally in macOS user preferences (UserDefaults) on your own Mac. They are never uploaded anywhere. Removing the app removes this data.

Permissions MACKey requests

Accessibility — required by macOS so MACKey can register global keyboard shortcuts and bring apps to the front. It is used solely for that purpose, entirely on-device. MACKey does not log your keystrokes and records nothing outside the shortcut-recording field.

MACKey reads your Dock's app list (to offer Dock-order shortcuts) directly from your local system preferences. This stays on your Mac.

Network

MACKey makes no network requests of any kind. It works fully offline.

Third parties

None. No third-party SDKs, no third-party servers, no data sharing.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new date.
